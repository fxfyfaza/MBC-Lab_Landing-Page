MBC Laboratory

MBC Lab is a Tech-Research Center and a Company Engaged in Cybersecurity, Big Data, Game-Tech, and Geographic Information System (GIS) Consulting.

About

About Our

The Multimedia Application, Big Data and Cyber Security Laboratory, located at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Telkom University and operating under the Networking, Cybernetics and Engineering Management (NCM) Research Group, is a specialized research facility with a multi-faceted focus.

Primarily, it studies the field of multimedia applications, particularly Game Technology and Geographic Information Systems (GIS), which utilize cutting-edge technologies to explore interactive simulations and spatial data analysis.

In addition, the lab is actively involved in Big Data efforts, specializing in Machine Learning techniques for extensive data analysis and insights.

In addition, its cybersecurity division is divided into three distinct teams: Red Team, which conducts cyberattack simulations for security assessments; Blue Team, responsible for improving defense systems; and Cloud Team, dedicated to securing cloud-based data and applications. This multifaceted laboratory is at the forefront of research and development in these important areas.
