About CyberSecurity

Cyber security is a practice to protect technological systems and devices such as computers, cellular, servers, electronic systems and data of a company. Maybe this profession still sounds unfamiliar, but every company will certainly do many ways to avoid cyber crime.

About Our CyberSecurity

Our cybersecurity division is divided into three different teams: Red Team, which conducts cyberattack simulations for security assessments; Blue Team, responsible for improving defense systems; and Cloud Team, dedicated to securing cloud-based data and applications.

About BigData

Big data is a very important concept in the world of information technology and modern business. In its application, big data refers to large and complex volumes of various types of data that are generated rapidly from multiple sources.

About Our BigData

Our big data division has 3 main focuses, such as Data Analysts, Data Scientists and Data Engineers, the core of which is to Process Data so that it is easy for others to understand.

About Game-Tech

Game technology, or gametech, refers to dedicated solutions and services that are used throughout a game's lifecycle and are built especially for the purpose of game development. This is in contrast to products which can be used by game developers but weren't built with them in mind.

About Our Game-Tech

Development that includes anything needed to actually build the game, such as engines, software management tools, art tools, localization, middleware, and audio tools

About GIS

GIS connects data to a map, integrating location data with all types of descriptive information. This provides a foundation for mapping and analysis that is used in science and almost every industry. GIS helps users understand patterns, relationship, and geographic context.

About Our GIS

Our gis division has 3 Focuses, such as Mobile Applications and UI/UX, 3D Character Modeling and Augment Reality, and also Remote Sensing.
